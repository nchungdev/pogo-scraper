        PvP Analysis

        Lugia was previously a staple in the Master League while also finding use in the lower PvP Leagues. GBL Season 20 brought about an energy nerf to Sky Attack, Lugia's go-to Charged attack, and now, it's a shadow of its former self ğŸ“‰.

        Costing only 50 energy previously, it now requires 55 energy, despite hitting harder. Overall, Lugia is less intimidating but still retains some viability.

        ğŸ”µ Great League - Lugia's USP is its ability to tank attacks, which is the primary reason for its use in PvP. With few heavy hitters in this format, Lugia often dominates, although its offensive capabilities are lacking ğŸ¥².

        ğŸŸ¡ Ultra League - The widespread presence of PokÃ©mon like Giratina, Melmetal, and several Dark types makes the Ultra League challenging for Lugia. It excels at absorbing damage but struggles to find enough offensive openings.

        ğŸŸ£ Master League - Lugia was once a terror in the Master League, and while it still poses a challenge to most PokÃ©mon, the Sky Attack nerf and the emergence of powerhouses like Dawn Wings Necrozma are gradually pushing it down the ranks.

        It's best used as a safe switch, even when facing high neutral damage. Also, if left with sufficient HP, it can serve as a strong closer ğŸ™‚.
