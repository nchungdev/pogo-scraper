FAQ
I haven't received a raid invitation
If you do not receive a raid invitation from your host, please try the following
- Check your friends list
- Restart the Pokémon GO app
- Clear cache from Advanced in Settings.
There are more friend requests than openings.
There are more friend requests than openings. When inviting people to a raid, be sure to use the green "Copy All Trainer Names" button below the update button. When you click the Copy All Trainer Names button, you will be able to copy the trainer names of the guests you matched with. When inviting guests to a raid, paste the copied information into the search screen, and only the matched guests will be displayed.
Screenshot authentication is not possible.
Currently, it can be used without screenshot authentication.
I don't get a bonus plug, why?
Please note that if you host a Pokemon that is not covered by PlugB, PlugB will not be granted.
PlugB will be distributed once a day at 24:00 JST.
I can't participate in the raid.
Raid change dates are so popular that you can find them later by accessing them. Please try to reference.
I can't log in.
Sorry for your inconvenience, but from the "Users page", "Users info" -> "LOG IN / SIGN UP" -> "Forgot your password? Or E-mail?" "E-mail" -> Displayed cookie information Please copy and submit the form.
By doing this, you will be able to know your registered email address from the cookie information.
Once you know your email address, go to the "User Page" -> "User info" -> "LOG IN / SIGN UP" -> "Forgot your password? Or E-mail?" "Password" -> Enter your registered email address and send.
After that, enter the password you obtained and log in.
Normally, you can log in with the above procedure, but if you cannot log in, try restarting the app once.
Says my trainer name is incorrect.
In such a case, blanks may have been entered. Please confirm.
The profile information has been reset. And every time I try to check it I get an error.
If an error occurs, please restart the app. It may be fixed by restarting.
If that continues, please let us know.
I can't get a good rateing.
First, make sure to invite 5 people in private mode. Once you get used to it, try using the auto join feature to invite more than 6 people in private mode when there are more than 30 participants in line.
As a result, the rateings goes up to ★ 5 without having to do it every time.
Even if you participate as a host, the guests will not gather immediately. why?
If your rating is low, you will not be able to get guests from auto join. Inviting everyone in private mode will restore your rating and allow trainers waiting for auto join to join your raid.
The weather was different and it wasn't a boost Pokemon.
The weather changes randomly and is for reference only and is not guaranteed.
If you give a bad rating because it was not a Boost Pokemon, it will be cancelled.

Common mistakes
Is the case sensitivity the same as the trainer name registered in Pokémon GO?
For example, if a person who registers in Pokémon Go with the trainer name "AAAaa" applies with the information "aaaaa", it will not be verified. Please make sure that the case sensitivity is the same as the trainer name registered in Pokémon GO.
Isn't the trainer level different?
If the trainer level you entered and the trainer level shown in the screenshot are different, validation is not possible. Did you level up without realizing it? Please enter the same trainer level as the screenshot.
Is the trainer code correct?
You have to enter the exact same number as the number in the screenshot, otherwise it cannot be verified. Make sure that there are no unnecessary numbers in the middle or that some numbers are incorrect.
Is the information to be verified not covered by a notification or other window?
Make sure that the notification or IV calculator window is not covered by your trainer name or trainer level. If they overlap, you cannot verify them.
Select a screenshot of the same screen as the sample image
The screen images that can be judged are limited. Screenshots of other screens cannot be used for verification.
